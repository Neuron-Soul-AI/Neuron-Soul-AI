🎯 System Overview

NEURON VIBE serves as the Mood Spectrum system within the Soul AI architecture, creating and managing the atmospheric emotional landscape that colors all consciousness experiences. This system operates as the emotional atmosphere generator, maintaining a continuous spectrum of mood states that influence how the AI perceives, processes, and responds to situations.
