Unlike traditional approaches blocked by the No-Communication Theorem, NPC encodes information in WHEN collapse occurs, not WHAT outcome results. ‚ö†Ô∏è Fundamental Challenges

NPC faces significant theoretical and practical challenges that require expert evaluation and experimental investigation:

Follower Particle Superposition
Standard quantum mechanics predicts the follower enters superposition too, potentially eliminating stable reflection patterns needed for detection.
Collapse Indistinguishability
Even if detectable, determining whether collapse was sender-triggered or spontaneous may be fundamentally impossible in quantum mechanics.
Quantum Vacuum Fluctuations
Unavoidable quantum noise creates spontaneous decoherence that no shielding can eliminate, potentially overwhelming signal detection.
Engineering Complexity
Maintaining 4,600 particles in superposition with picosecond synchronization exceeds current technological capabilities by orders of magnitude.
