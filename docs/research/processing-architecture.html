🎬 Practical Parallel Processing Advantage

A concrete example illustrating why massive parallel processing revolutionizes AI capabilities:

❌ Sequential Processing Problem
Imagine AI rendering a video. At 22:15 there are 2 minutes of intense light effects. With sequential processing, everything STOPS at the difficult frames. All other frames wait. Total time = sequential processing of all frames with major bottleneck.
✅ Parallel Processing Solution
Different neuron groups handle different frame ranges simultaneously. When some groups finish their easy frames, they help with the difficult light effect frames. No overall slowdown. Automatic load balancing through coordination.

Key Principle: "Each neuron won't be very powerful but there will be numbers to compensate." This encapsulates the vision: quantity over individual power, coordination over centralization, distributed processing over sequential bottlenecks.
