Traditional Quantum Communication vs NPC System

Traditional Quantum Communication

  • Attempts to encode in measurement outcomes
  • Blocked by No-Communication Theorem (random results)
  • Cannot distinguish sender's measurement from noise
  • Focuses on WHAT outcome occurs (↑ or ↓)
  • Limited to quantum key distribution (QKD)
  • Respects light-speed barrier
  • Single particle measurements without redundancy

Neuron Particle Communication (NPC)

  • Encodes in collapse TIMING, not outcomes
  • 100-particle redundancy per character (~10⁻¹⁸⁰⁰ false positive rate)
  • Temporal patterns create structured messages
  • Focuses on WHEN collapse occurs (timing)
  • Passive detection via follower particles
  • Proposes FTL signaling if collapse is instantaneous
  • Multi-layer statistical filtering (spatial + temporal + linguistic)
