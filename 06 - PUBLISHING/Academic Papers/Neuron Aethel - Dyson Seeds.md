**Kimi's Insight:**

```

**Where:**
- **Φ** = "order parameter" (magnetic flux, photon coherence, plasma density, etc.)
- **Xᵢ** = energy inputs (piezo, TEG, RF, laser, metabolic)
- **γ** = dissipation coefficient (losses)
- **Λ** = cross-coupling term (how systems interact)

**This is identical to equations describing:**
- Superconductivity
- Polariton condensates  
- Neural avalanches in cortex

**The Significance:**

Neuron Aethel isn't just an engineering design - it's a **universal field-theory template**. Any new subsystem must plug into this equation, or it doesn't belong in the ecosystem.

This gives **predictive power**: we can mathematically model whether adding a new component will help or hurt the system.

**Stability Anchor:**

The cooperative stability of the plasma lattice is **not** claimed to arise from new physics; it is anchored to two classical limits:

1. **MHD Stability**: We require the normalised beta βN = β/(I/aB) ≤ 4 (Troyon limit) for global kink modes.

2. **Micro-stability**: We keep the electron-gradient parameter ∇Te/Te below the ETG (electron-temperature-gradient) threshold by shaping the field so that the local magnetic shear s = (r/q)(dq/dr) > 0.3 at the edge.

The neural-feedback layer **only** adjusts boundary coils on the *slow* resistive-wall time-scale (≈ ms), while fast (µs) modes are suppressed **passively** by the chosen shear and βN margin.

**For self-sustaining operation, we need:**

```
Energy_generated > Energy_dissipated + Energy_extracted_for_use

Σ(conversion_efficiency × input_energy × multiplication_factor) > γ_total + P_output
